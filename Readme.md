## ğŸ” Key Findings

### Content Distribution
Netflix's library demonstrates a clear preference for **Movies over TV Shows**, reflecting a strategy focused on single-watch entertainment experiences rather than episodic content.

### Growth Trajectory
Content additions experienced **explosive growth post-2015**, with peak expansion occurring between **2018-2020**, marking Netflix's aggressive content acquisition phase.

### Genre Landscape
**Drama** and **Comedy** emerge as the platform's pillars, dominating the genre distribution and appealing to broad audience demographics.

### Global Production
The **United States** leads content production, followed by **India** and the **United Kingdom**.

### Audience Focus
Rating analysis reveals concentration in **TV-MA** and **TV-14** categories, indicating strategic targeting of **teenage and adult audiences** over family-friendly content.

### Duration Standards
Movie runtimes cluster between **80-120 minutes**, adhering to traditional cinema standards and optimal viewer engagement windows.
